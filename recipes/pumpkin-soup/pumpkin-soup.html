Pumpkin soup

Description

While it looks elegant and is an appealing addition to a holiday meal, this creamy soup is so simple to make. My husband was skeptical at first, but after one bowl, he asked for second helpings! â€”Elizabeth Montgomery, Allston, Massachusetts

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose floor
  • 2 cans (14-1/2 ounces each) chicken broth
  • 1 can (15 ounces) solid-pack pumpkin
  • 1 teaspoon brown sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • 1/8 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1 cup heavy whipped cream

Steps

  1. In a large saucepan, saute onion in butter until tender. Remove from the heat; stir in flour until smooth.
  2. Gradually stir in the broth, pumpkin, brown sugar, salt, pepper and nutmeg; bring to a boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes.
  3. Add cream; cook until heated through, about 2 minutes.
